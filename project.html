Shipping Company Data Management System:

There are customers, administor, shippers, package managers and couriers in the system. Customers are able to send their packages to their desired destinations by either calling a courier to their own location to collect the package or submitting the package to any branch in person. A package manager works in a branch and transfers packages to destination branches and assigns packages transferred from other branches to couriers or shippers to deliver. For malformed packages, customers are able to decline the shipping and file a report about it. Complaints about lost packages are also considered.
